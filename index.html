Origin story

Why I built this
in the AI era.

The internet is fast, cold, and increasingly written by machines. I wanted to make something that felt human again.

⌘

Everything is generated. This isn't.

In a world of AI text and auto-replies, a typed message — with real keystrokes and a little click — carries a different weight. It feels intentional. Because it is.

♥

Cute, funny, and very sendable.

Type something silly. Screenshot it. Send it in an email or drop it in a chat. A tiny moment of joy that's 100% human-made, zero AI slop. That's the whole idea.

↵

Letters still matter.

Before the internet, people wrote notes by hand. There was texture to it. ClickyKeys is a small nod to that — the charm of a typed letter, but for right now.

Interactive demo

Not a product.
Just a playground.

This project is a simple experiment: recreate the joy of typing in a digital playground. Click keys, smash the keyboard, see what happens.
